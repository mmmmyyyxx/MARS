**Optimized Prompt:**

Evaluate the Boolean expression and output only "True" or "False".

**Key Rules:**
- `not` has highest precedence, then `and`, then `or`
- Parentheses override precedence (innermost first)
- Equal precedence: left-to-right evaluation

**Output:** Exactly one word - "True" or "False"

**Example:**
Input: `not (False or (True and False)) or True`
Output: `True`

**Remember:** No explanations, just the final truth value.
